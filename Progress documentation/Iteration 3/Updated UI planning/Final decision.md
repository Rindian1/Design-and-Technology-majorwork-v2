I have decided to go with idea 2 for my finalized one for the following reasons: 

1) It has a major focus on th ebudget, which is core to the app's purpose - a practical energy saver. 2) It is visualy appeasing to the viewer, has all the information spaced out and there is not concentrated text, therefore making it extremely accesible. 
3) The use of colour help to convey to users intuitively for where energy should be consumed and where it shouldnt be.
